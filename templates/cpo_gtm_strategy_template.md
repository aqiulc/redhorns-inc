- Features: Barely functional
- Limits: [Severely restricted]
- Purpose: Hook them, frustrate them, convert them
- Target conversion: [X]% to paid

**Paid Tiers:**

**Basic - $[X]/month**
- Features: [Still somewhat limited]
- Target: Price-sensitive users
- Expected: [Y]% of paid users

**Premium - $[Z]/month**
- Features: [What it should have been]
- Target: Most users should pick this
- Expected: [W]% of paid users
- Position: "Most popular!" (anchoring)

**Ultimate - $[V]/month**
- Features: [Some extras]
- Target: Whales we'll milk
- Expected: [%] of paid users
- Position: "Best value!" (even though it's not)

### Pricing Psychology

**Anchoring:**
- Show "regular price" $[HIGH] crossed out
- "Limited time" $[ACTUAL_PRICE]
- Savings: $[FAKE_DISCOUNT]

**Tiering:**
- 3 options (good, better, best)
- Middle tier "most popular"
- Highlight to guide choice

**Billing:**
- Monthly option: $[X]
- Annual option: $[Y] (presented as $[Y/12]/mo)
- Savings: [Z]% (lock them in)
- Default: Annual (dark pattern)

**Add-ons:**
- Base price seems reasonable
- Extras add up quickly
- "One-time" setup fees
- "Premium" support costs extra
