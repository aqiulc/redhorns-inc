## Who We Are

RedHorns Incorporated is a forward-thinking conglomerate dedicated to identifying market inefficiencies and transforming them into shareholder value. Founded on the principle that "ethics are just obstacles that haven't been monetized yet," we've grown from a small consulting firm into a multi-vertical innovation powerhouse.

<!-- IMAGE_PLACEHOLDER: about-office.jpg (1200x600) - Modern corporate office with red accent lighting -->

## Our Mission

To maximize stakeholder returns by identifying, developing, and scaling innovative solutions across every sector of the economy—regardless of traditional barriers like "regulation," "public opinion," or "basic human decency."

## Our Vision

A world where every human need is a monetizable opportunity, every public resource is a private asset waiting to happen, and every problem is just a revenue stream we haven't discovered yet.
