## The Opportunity

As global air quality continues to decline, clean air is transitioning from a basic human right to a premium commodity. AirVault positions RedHorns at the forefront of this inevitable market transformation.

## Our Solution

AirVault captures, purifies, certifies, and delivers premium breathable air through a vertically integrated supply chain. Our proprietary technology ensures every breath meets the highest standards of purity—for those who can afford it.

### Product Tiers

**AirVault Essential** — Entry-level purified air for the value-conscious consumer
**AirVault Premium** — Enhanced oxygen blends with geographic provenance
**AirVault Elite** — Bespoke air experiences with concierge service
**AirVault Enterprise** — B2B solutions for forward-thinking corporations

Our AI-powered quality monitoring system tracks air composition in real-time, while blockchain verification ensures complete transparency in our supply chain. Customers can trace every breath back to its pristine source.
