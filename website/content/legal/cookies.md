## We Use Cookies

This website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies. By using this site, you accept our cookie practices regardless of the tiny banner you probably ignored.

## Types of Cookies

### Essential Cookies
Required for basic functionality. You can't opt out because we said so.

### Analytics Cookies
Help us understand how you use our site, what you click, where you hesitate, and when you give up.

### Marketing Cookies
Enable personalized advertising so relevant ads follow you across the internet forever.

### Third-Party Cookies
Our partners also want to know everything about you.

## Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings, but this will break most functionality and we'll still find ways to track you.
