## Product Vision

**SecondPlate transforms food waste into accessible nutrition through a premium subscription experience that makes rescued food aspirational, not shameful.**

## Target Users

### Primary: Value-Conscious Consumers
- **Age:** 25-45
- **Income:** $25K-$45K household
- **Psychographics:** Budget-conscious, sustainability-minded, tech-savvy
- **Pain Points:** Food costs too much, wants to reduce waste, values convenience

### Secondary: Enterprise Buyers
- **Prisons:** Cost reduction on food budgets
- **Shelters:** Stretch limited resources
- **Colleges:** Appeal to sustainability-focused students

## Product Tiers

### Basic ($29/month)
- 4 rescued meals per week
- Standard delivery windows
- FreshScore >60 items
- Community pickup option

### Plus ($59/month)
- 8 rescued meals per week
- Priority delivery (2-hour windows)
- FreshScore >70 items
- Free delivery
- Skip/pause flexibility

### Premium ($99/month)
- Unlimited rescued meals
- Same-day delivery
- FreshScore >80 items (premium surplus)
- Concierge support
- Exclusive restaurant partnerships

## Core Features

### Restaurant Partner App
- **Quick List:** Photo → Auto-tag → Price → Submit (<60 seconds)
- **Earnings Dashboard:** Real-time revenue, waste savings
- **Smart Scheduling:** AI suggests optimal surplus times
- **Compliance Tracker:** Temperature logs, safety certifications

### Consumer App
- **Real-Time Feed:** Browse available food nearby
- **Smart Filters:** Dietary restrictions, cuisine preferences
- **One-Tap Order:** Streamlined checkout
- **Live Tracking:** See driver location
- **Impact Dashboard:** "You've saved 127 lbs of food!"

### Enterprise Portal
- **Bulk Ordering:** Schedule recurring deliveries
- **Nutrition Reports:** Compliance documentation
- **Budget Planning:** Forecast costs vs traditional suppliers
- **Quality Analytics:** Track FreshScore trends

## User Experience Principles

1. **Never Use "Leftover"** - Language is "rescued," "surplus," "premium seconds"
2. **Make It Aspirational** - Beautiful photography, influencer partnerships
3. **Transparency Builds Trust** - Show FreshScore, restaurant source, prep time
4. **Gamify Sustainability** - Badges, streaks, community leaderboards
5. **Reduce Friction** - One-tap ordering, saved preferences, smart defaults

## Key User Flows

### New Subscriber Onboarding
1. Download app → Sign up (email/phone)
2. Select dietary preferences (vegan, allergies, etc.)
3. Choose subscription tier + delivery address
4. Add payment method
5. **First box free** (hook users)
6. Push notification when first food available

### Restaurant Partner Onboarding
1. Submit partnership application
2. Virtual demo call (sales team)
3. Install app → Connect POS (optional)
4. Training video (3 minutes)
5. First surplus listing (guided)
6. Payout setup (Stripe Connect)

## Competitive Differentiation

| Competitor | Weakness | Our Advantage |
|------------|----------|---------------|
| Food Banks | Free but stigmatized, limited hours | Premium UX, subscription convenience |
| Discount Grocers | Still expensive | 25-60% deeper discounts |
| Meal Kits | $10-15/meal | $2-5/meal average |
| Delivery Apps | Full-price restaurant food | Rescued food at massive discount |

## Risk Mitigation

**"Eating garbage" perception:**
- Premium branding, influencer campaigns
- FreshScore transparency builds trust
- Celebrity chef partnerships ("rescued food recipes")

**Food safety concerns:**
- Over-communicate FreshScore methodology
- Offer money-back guarantee + insurance
- FDA compliance certifications
