### Unit Economics Deep Dive

**LTV Calculation Review:**
```
LTV = ARPU × Gross Margin × (1 / Churn Rate)
LTV = $112 × 74% × (1 / 5%) = $112 × 74% × 20 = $1,658
```

**LTV Assumptions:**
- ARPU: $112 - **REALISTIC** (weighted avg of $29/$99/$299 tiers)
- Gross Margin: 74% - **REALISTIC** (validated with actual vendor quotes)
- Churn: 5% - **REALISTIC** (industry benchmark)

**LTV Assessment:** Highly achievable. Conservative even.

CEO's $4,800 LTV assumes 4-year retention (48 months). Our $1,658 assumes 20 months. Reality will likely be somewhere in between - behavioral addiction mechanics suggest 30+ month retention is achievable.

**LTV Risk:** **LOW** - Downside protected, upside significant

---

**CAC Calculation Review:**
```
CAC = Marketing Spend / New Customers
CAC = $1.08M / 10,417 = $104
```

**CAC Assumptions:**
- Channel mix: 25% paid social, 24% influencer, 22% content, 29% referral
- Conversion rates: Validated from comparable wellness apps
- Organic: 30% of acquisition via referral (reduces blended CAC)

**CAC Assessment:** Achievable with disciplined channel management.

$104 blended CAC assumes strong referral program performance (30% of users). If referrals underperform, CAC rises to $140 - still acceptable given LTV.

**CAC Risk:** **MEDIUM** - Referral program critical, but has fallback channels
