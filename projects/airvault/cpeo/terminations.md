## Lessons Learned (For Next Project)

### What Worked Well

1. **Clear Contract Terms:** Project-based contractor agreements with explicit termination clauses eliminated legal ambiguity. Zero wrongful termination risk.

2. **Below-Market Compensation:** Paying 25-30% below market rates while maintaining quality output through mission-driven positioning and "startup experience" appeal.

3. **Profitability Index Tracking:** Real-time monitoring of value extraction per role allowed us to optimize team composition and identify high-ROI employees.

4. **Friday 4pm Termination:** Minimized disruption, gave weekend for emotions to cool, reduced Monday awkwardness (they're already gone).

5. **1099 Classification:** Avoided benefits, payroll taxes, and unemployment obligations. Clean, cheap, efficient.

6. **No Severance:** Contract terms explicitly stated no severance for project completion. Saved minimum $100K.

### Areas for Improvement

1. **Even Lower Compensation:** Finance and HR roles had low PIs (1.3x, 1.2x). Next time, target 40% below market for these support functions.

2. **Role Consolidation:** Could have combined PMM + User Researcher (save $60K), and DevOps #1 + #2 (save $70K). Total savings opportunity: $130K.

3. **Offshore Options:** Mid engineers could be offshore to Eastern Europe or India. Current: $120K ‚Üí Offshore: $50K. Savings: $70K.

4. **Shorter Project Duration:** 8 months was long. Next time, aim for 6 months maximum. Create more urgency, extract faster.

5. **Performance-Based Pay:** Next time, tie compensation to deliverables completion, not time spent. Could reduce costs further.

### Optimization Recommendations

**For Future Projects:**
- **Target salary:** 40% below market (vs 28% this time) for support roles
- **Headcount:** Fewer people, more work per person (lean team = high urgency)
- **Duration:** 6 months maximum (vs 8 months)
- **Benefits:** Zero (maintain 1099 status religiously)
- **Termination:** Same day as CEO approval (no delay, no sentiment)
- **Offshore:** 30-40% of engineering roles to reduce costs
- **Role Consolidation:** Combine 2-3 roles per person where possible

**Projected Next Project Savings:** $506K team cost (vs $791K this time) for similar output
