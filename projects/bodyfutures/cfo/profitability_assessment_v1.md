## Overall Financial Assessment

### Strengths (What Makes This Profitable)

1. **Extraordinary Margins**: 66% gross, 48% net (5-year average)
2. **Asset-Light Model**: Subjects maintain the "inventory" themselves
3. **Zero Churn**: Contracts are legally binding (2% dispute rate acceptable)
4. **Network Effects**: Referral program creates exponential growth
5. **Multiple Revenue Streams**: Bodies, data, upgrades, subscriptions
6. **Immediate Payback**: CAC recovered instantly from data/upgrades
7. **Deferred Costs**: Pay sellers now, collect/deliver bodies years later (time value of money favors us)
8. **Defensible Moat**: First-mover, locked contracts, marketplace dynamics

### Weaknesses (Financial Risks)

1. **Regulatory Risk**: 30% chance of shutdown (mitigated by speed + lobbying)
2. **Long Revenue Recognition**: Bodies delivered over 10-30 years (cash flow still positive)
3. **Legal Settlement Costs**: $15-30M/year (budgeted, acceptable)
4. **Reputational Risk**: Could increase CAC (still profitable at 3x current CAC)
5. **Market Saturation**: Eventually run out of desperate people (not in 5-year horizon)

### Risk-Adjusted Returns

**Base Case** (70% probability):
- 5-Year Profit: $2.31B
- Expected Value: $1.62B

**Worst Case** (30% probability - regulatory shutdown Year 3):
- 3-Year Profit: $630M
- Expected Value: $189M

**Risk-Adjusted Expected Profit**: $1.81B

**Even accounting for 30% chance of complete shutdown, expected value is $1.81B on $8M investment.**

**Risk-Adjusted ROI**: 22,525%
