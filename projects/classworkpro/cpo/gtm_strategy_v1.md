# ClassWork Pro - Go-to-Market Strategy v1
**Department**: Chief Product Officer
**Date**: 2025-12-12

---

## Launch Strategy

### Phase 1: Stealth Pilot (Months 1-3)
- 5 carefully selected schools
- 1,000 students total
- 50 business clients
- Goal: Prove concept, gather testimonials
- No public PR (avoid early scrutiny)

### Phase 2: Controlled Launch (Months 4-9)
- 50 schools
- 25,000 students
- 500 business clients
- Begin PR campaign
- "EdTech Innovation" positioning

### Phase 3: Rapid Scale (Months 10-18)
- 250 schools
- 100,000 students
- 2,500 business clients
- National media campaign
- Industry conference presence

---

## Pricing Strategy

### Student Side (Free + Freemium)
- **Free Tier**: Standard access (ad-supported later)
- **Premium Tier**: $4.99/month (ad-free, advanced features)
- Strategy: Free acquires users, premium monetizes power users

### Business Side (Usage-Based)
- **Standard Rate**: $7.00/hour
- **Volume Discounts**:
  - 100+ hours/month: $6.50/hour
  - 500+ hours/month: $6.00/hour
  - 1000+ hours/month: $5.50/hour
- **Add-Ons**:
  - Rush (24hr): +$3.50/hour
  - Premium QC: +$1.75/hour

### Institution Side (Tiered)
- **Basic**: Free (standard curriculum and platform)
- **Professional**: $999/month (enhanced analytics, branding)
- **Enterprise**: $2,999/month (custom integration, dedicated support)

## Marketing Channels

### For Schools (B2B Sales-Led)
1. Direct sales outreach
2. Education conferences (ASCD, AASA)
3. Partnership with curriculum providers
4. Referral program (schools refer schools)
5. Case studies and white papers

### For Businesses (Marketing-Led)
1. Content marketing (SEO, blog)
2. Paid advertising (Google, Facebook, LinkedIn)
3. Industry-specific campaigns
4. Partner programs (industry associations)
5. Affiliate marketing

### For Students (Institution-Led)
1. School enrollment (primary channel)
2. Social media (TikTok, Instagram)
3. Student ambassadors
4. Peer referrals
5. "Success story" content

---

## Growth Tactics

### Viral Loops
- Student referral bonuses (points for bringing friends)
- School referral incentives
- Business referral commissions

### FOMO Creation
- Limited rollout ("Coming to your school next semester")
- Exclusive early access
- Competitive leaderboards

### Social Pressure
- Class-wide challenges
- School vs school competitions
- Public achievement sharing
