## Break-Even Analysis

### Monthly Break-Even (Steady State)

**Fixed Costs (Monthly)**
- Team: $350K
- Infrastructure: $42K
- Overhead: $100K
- **Total Fixed**: $492K/month

**Variable Costs per Hour**
- QC overhead: $0.15/hour
- Support: $0.05/hour
- Infrastructure: $0.03/hour
- **Total Variable**: $0.23/hour

**Contribution Margin**
- Revenue per hour: $7.00
- Variable cost per hour: $0.23
- **Contribution**: $6.77/hour

**Break-Even Hours per Month**
- Fixed costs / Contribution margin
- $492K / $6.77 = 72,700 hours/month

**Break-Even Students**
- Hours per student per month: ~20 hours (22 school days)
- Students needed: 72,700 / 20 = 3,635 students

**We break even at 3,635 students. We're targeting 100,000 Year 1.**

## Financial Risks & Mitigation

### Revenue Risks

**Risk**: Student dropout rates higher than projected
- **Impact**: Lower labor supply = lower revenue
- **Mitigation**: Penalty system keeps retention >90%
- **Financial Buffer**: Conservative case still profitable

**Risk**: Business client churn due to quality issues
- **Impact**: Lost revenue, reputation damage
- **Mitigation**: Strong QC, free revisions, realistic task matching
- **Financial Buffer**: High margins allow for quality investment

**Risk**: Pricing pressure (competitors undercut us)
- **Impact**: Lower revenue per hour
- **Mitigation**: Quality differentiation, lock-in effects, unique positioning
- **Financial Buffer**: Can reduce to $5/hour and still maintain 80% margins

### Cost Risks

**Risk**: QC costs higher than projected
- **Impact**: Lower margins
- **Mitigation**: Invest in AI automation, efficient processes
- **Financial Buffer**: Even doubling QC costs still yields 85% margins

**Risk**: Legal defense costs
- **Impact**: One-time or ongoing legal expenses
- **Mitigation**: Strong initial legal structure, insurance, reserves
- **Financial Buffer**: $5M annual legal budget built into Year 2+ projections

**Risk**: Regulatory compliance costs
- **Impact**: New requirements increase overhead
- **Mitigation**: Proactive compliance, lobbying, legal structure flexibility
- **Financial Buffer**: Margins so high we can absorb significant compliance costs

### Macro Risks

**Risk**: Economic recession reduces business demand
- **Impact**: Lower client acquisition, reduced usage
- **Mitigation**: We're cheaper than alternatives (recession-resilient)
- **Financial Buffer**: Student supply remains stable (schools need us)

**Risk**: Education budget cuts affect school partnerships
- **Impact**: Slower institutional adoption
- **Mitigation**: We save schools money (counter-cyclical)
- **Financial Buffer**: Can offer revenue share to incentivize adoption
